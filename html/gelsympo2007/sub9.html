　Excursion
Tokyo
Tour departs from Hongo campus at 13:00 on August 8 and visits to the following places. The bus returns to the campus around 21:00.

(1) National Diet Building
-----The Japanese Capitol (the House of Parliament).
(2) Imperial Palace Plaza
-----Short photo stop at the outer garden of the Palace.
(3) ASAKUSA KANNON Temple
-----The oldest and most popular Buddhist temple in Tokyo.
(4) NAKAMISE shopping arcade
-----A colorful shopping arcade leading to the temple, lined with tiny food and souvenir shops.
(5) SUMIDA river cruise
-----Enjoy about 1.5 hour boat ride (Yakata-bune; traditional Japanese style boat) from Asakusa to Tokyo Bay with dinner. The river was the most important waterway for the development of Tokyo.

Excursion Fee : 15,000 JPY
To apply, please check the checkbox for excursion in the registration form.
